## WebP

To use WebP and make JPEG as backup

```html
<picture>
  <source srcset="img/image.webp" type="image/webp">
  <source srcset="img/image.jpg" type="image/jpeg">
  <img src="img/image.jpg" alt="Alt Text!">
</picture>
```
